learn somali v0
purpose: to assist in structuring and learning somali. there are a lack of online resources to learn afroasiatic languages.
target audience: east african diaspora, anyone interested in learning a cushitic language
v0 is just the front end design
v1 will be a translator/resource app, incorporate gemini to assist with translation, react/tailwind/mongodb with user auth/sign-in to store notes
future goals: include other cushitic/afroasiatic languages such as amharic, oromo, afar, beja, tigrinia
